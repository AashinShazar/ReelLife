CookieMonster

You already do the hard work making the content. Let us help you highlight it.

What is CookieMonster?

Creating content is hard. Editing it is even harder. Save time by finding the most impactful parts of your visual content that will help you reach a larger audience.

Use CookieMonster's optimized algorithm that allows you to pick out the best of your photos, movies, ads and vlogs that'll have the most impact on your audience.

Who's it for?

Any and every visual content creator. We aspire to branch out to all mediums of content but for now focus on images

Examples of CookieMonster users:

  • Instagram influencer selecting the best snapshot to post that will have the highest conversion advertising a product
  • Pinterest or Reddit Subreddit user who want's to post themed content pictures that serve to highlight a subject
  • Movie industry professional seeking to make trailers, promo material (posters, insta posts, tweets and online ad campaigns)
  • Bloggers, magazines and photographers covering issues/events

Software eats the world

You give us the set of pictures or videos that you'd like the highlights of, and CookieMonster returns a set of curated photos to you in a specified folder. That's the hope for a future iteration. For now, we enjoy the simpler times.

Paste the YouTube link into the search bar above and you get a folder of selectively curated photos.

CookieMonster is trained on Gigabytes of video frames that represent both 'good' and 'bad' scenes. Our model runs your frames against our multi-filtered model and then returns the images that are the 'best'
